Leviathan's Gauntlet

Leviathan’s Gauntlet is a fast-paced, wave-based pirate combat game where players take command of a large ship and battle against relentless fleets of different enemies in a stylised, cartoonish ocean world.

Sail through increasingly intense waves of foes, using a combination of abilities, upgrades, and precise movement to survive the gauntlet. After each wave, dock at the shop island to repair and upgrade to prepare for the next challenge.

With a blend of arcade combat, strategic progression, and a hint of eldritch mystery lurking beneath the waves, Leviathan’s Gauntlet delivers bite-sized action with high replayability and pirate-flavoured chaos.

Features

  • Engaging Ship Movement
  • Multiple Waves of Enemies
  • Ship Abilities and Upgrades
  • A Shop System to Upgrade The Ship and Abilities
  • Ship Currency
  • Multiple Enemy Types
  • Gold Pickups
  • Entertaining World Borders
